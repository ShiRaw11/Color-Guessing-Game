🕹️ How to Play

Welcome to the Color Guessing Game! Ready to test your memory and reflexes? 🎨🧠

🎯 Objective: Guess the correct color before time runs out!

🔹 A Target color appears for a few seconds.
⏳ The color disappears, and you must remember it!
🎨 Select the correct color from multiple choices.
✔️ A correct guess earns 1 point and moves you forward.
❌ A wrong guess or timeout ends the game!
🔥 Higher levels = more colors & more challenge!

Let’s go! 🚀
